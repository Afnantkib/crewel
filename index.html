Our roots

The society has done a lot of awareness programmes workshops in different areas of district kupwara. The society has also done lots of work in eradicating evils like illetracy ,dowry,poverty etc. The society has participated in several national level exhibitions. The society has been running cluster in different villages in district kupwara. The society is aiming to work in other fields also to help poor people and serve the nation.

In Handicrafts sector the society has been working actively to benefit the poor artisans of kupwara providing them the platform to showcase their skills . The society provides all the necessities to the artisans of district kupwara so that they earn their livelihood. The society provides raw materials yarn threads cloth material to artisans to make it easy for them ,as these things are not readily available in the District.
